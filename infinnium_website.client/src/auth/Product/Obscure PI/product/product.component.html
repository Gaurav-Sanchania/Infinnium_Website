ObscurePI®

ObscurePI®, a key module of the 4iG Suite, is an advanced technology focusing on the identification, management, and protection of personally identifiable information (PII) through various automated actions such as redaction, anonymization, deletion or encryption. ObscurePI® is a first of kind technology to offer on-demand redactions on all native file types eliminating the need to image documents in order to apply redactions.

TRUST

ObscurePI® simplifies privacy, and compliance – and enables the sound data stewardship that builds trust over time.

COMPLIANCE

As our moral, ethical and legal obligations to identify and protect PII continues to increase, ObscurePI® enables the identification, regulatory compliance and protection of PII required by modern business privacy and compliance needs.

EFFICIENCY

This substantially simplifies compliance with HIPAA, GDPR, CCPA, Sarbanes-Oxley, FISMA and other data privacy and compliance regulations.

Features

Cohesive Search & Discovery

Easily locate and retrieve relevant data across the organization through a unified search interface that streamlines information discovery.

Geolocation of Content Based on Identified PII

Automatically detect and map content containing Personally Identifiable Information (PII) to its geographic location for compliance and risk management.

Comprehensive Audit and Reporting

Generate detailed, customizable audit trails and reports to track data access, modifications, and compliance activities.

Automated Redaction of Both PII and Privileged Information

Protect sensitive data with automated redaction of PII and privileged information across documents and records.

Comprehensive Export Options

Export data in multiple formats, ensuring seamless compatibility with various external systems and regulatory requirements.

Many 3rd Party Integrations

Connect with a wide range of third-party tools and services to enhance workflow, compliance, and data management capabilities.

Recover Actionable Intelligence from Data

Extract valuable insights and actionable intelligence from structured and unstructured data sources within the platform.

Data Tagging, Tracking, and Security

Classify, monitor, and secure data with advanced tagging, tracking, and security features to ensure proper governance and compliance.

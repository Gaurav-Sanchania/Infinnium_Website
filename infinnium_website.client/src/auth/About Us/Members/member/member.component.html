Charles Lavallée

Director, Business Development

Charles joins Infinnium with an extensive background in e-Discovery and Compliance software solutions that leverage AI and cloud-based architecture. With over 20 years in legal technology sales, Charles is highly recognized by his clients for his knowledge in practice and technology.

He brings a demonstrated devotion to building better partnerships with his clients and a focus on helping them drive new revenue. He has worked with corporations, consultancies, legal service providers, government agencies, and top law firms around the world.

Originally from the Washington, DC area, Charles has lived in NYC and abroad. From startups to Fortune 500 companies, he has always evangelized better technology and processes for his clients. A true international man, he has spent much of his career introducing new technologies in the UK, EU, and Canada as well as the Asia/Australia markets.

Charles holds a BA in Political Science and French from Providence College, studied International Law & Economics at the Université de Fridbourg, Switzerland, and participated at the former University of Dubrovnik Academy for International Affairs and Economics. A FAA Part 107 commercial drone pilot and an avid snowboarder, Charles and his wife Jennifer also enjoy travel and seeking new adventures.
